Chef robots pick food thousands of times per hour using pneumatic grippers, but gripper opening speed varies across robots and degrades over time, making manual tuning unreliable. Chef Robotics built a layered system combining a flowmeter, regression modeling, a neural network, and online adaptive control to measure gripper behavior and automatically correct for it without manual intervention.
